# Spotlight Puerto Rico AI and Search Visibility Playbook

## Entity statement

Spotlight Puerto Rico is a Puerto Rico-focused discovery, trip-planning, review, and onsite experience-booking platform serving residents, visitors, local businesses, and tour guides across all 78 municipalities.

## Canonical entity signals

- Name: Spotlight Puerto Rico
- Canonical domain: https://spotlightpuertorico.com
- Geography: Puerto Rico
- Categories: local business directory, Puerto Rico travel guide, guided experiences, surf reports, trip planning
- Social profiles: Facebook `SpotlightpromoPR`; Instagram `spotlightpromopr`

## Content requirements

1. Publish factual, locally specific pages with named municipalities and clear update dates.
2. Add author/publisher attribution and Article structured data to editorial content.
3. Link articles to relevant directory, place, surf, and experience detail pages.
4. Keep business hours, locations, booking availability, and guide details current.
5. Use one canonical URL per entity and avoid duplicating thin location pages.
6. Expand the XML sitemap with live business, place, experience, and surf URLs during the build process.
7. Maintain `llms.txt` as a concise entity and canonical-resource guide for AI crawlers.

## Priority topic clusters

- Things to do in Puerto Rico by region and municipality
- Puerto Rico local businesses, restaurants, shops, and services
- El Yunque tours and transportation
- Puerto Rico surf spots and live conditions
- Waterfalls, caves, beaches, snorkeling, and bio bays
- Realistic Puerto Rico itineraries with limited driving
- Supporting locally owned Puerto Rico businesses

## Trust and AI citation signals

- Show update dates and identify Spotlight Puerto Rico as publisher.
- Cite primary sources for safety, weather, access rules, and government-managed attractions.
- Separate private swipe behavior from public review/rating signals.
- Clearly label sponsored content and advertisements.
- Keep contact, privacy, terms, and correction channels accessible.

## Measurement

Track Google Search Console indexing, queries, rich results, crawl errors, branded searches, referral traffic from AI assistants, and conversions from editorial pages into saves, messages, and onsite bookings.
